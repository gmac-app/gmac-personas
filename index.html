GMAC customer experience map

Teresa Torres methodology: Each moment of friction and struggle is a candidate need — the opportunity space. The map shows current reality, not ideal future. Grounded in 10 research sources across 4,253+ survey respondents, 17 interview transcripts, 13.5M GA4 users, and 4 product studies.
